Home

Welcome to Troop 52 in Palo Alto! Our scouts learn outdoor skills, leadership, and teamwork while having fun with friends. From campouts to service projects, we challenge ourselves to grow and help our community.

Camping

On a typical campout, scouts work together to set up tents, cook meals, and build safe campfires. Everyone has a job, and we support each other to make camp run smoothly. At night, we share stories by the fire and enjoy the quiet outdoors.

Hiking

Hiking helps our scouts explore new trails and appreciate nature. We learn map reading, trail safety, and how to stay prepared on long walks. Along the way, we spot wildlife, enjoy scenic views, and build confidence step by step.

Service Projects

Service is an important part of Troop 52. Our scouts help with park cleanups, neighborhood improvement days, and local volunteering events. Through these projects, we practice kindness, responsibility, and giving back to Palo Alto.

Patrols

Propane Turtles

The Propane Turtles are known for staying calm and organized on every outing. They focus on strong camp skills, especially cooking and campsite setup. Their teamwork helps newer scouts learn quickly and feel included.

Vipers

The Vipers are energetic and always ready for adventure on hikes and campouts. They challenge themselves with new skills like knot tying and first aid. Their patrol spirit keeps everyone motivated and having fun.
