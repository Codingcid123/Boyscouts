Latest Troop Updates

Border Reviews

Several Scouts met the annual border review this year. Congratulations to all of the scouts that acquired new ranks!

Upcoming May Fete Parade (May 2 2026)

Scouts will have the chance to walk our banner in the annual May Fete Parade. Our troop have partook in this parade in past years and are planning to run it back again, with the addition of one more race cart.

Upcoming backpacking trip at Monte Bello Open Space Preserve (May 16-17)

Scouts will have the exciting opportunity to partake in a 1-night backpacking trip at Monte Bello Open Space Preserve. We hope they have a great time exploring the wildlife and beautiful scenery.
