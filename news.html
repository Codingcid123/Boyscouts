Latest Troop Updates

Spring Campout Success

Scouts worked in teams to pitch tents, cook dinner, and run campfire skits. New scouts finished their first overnight campout and learned basic campsite setup.

Merit Badge Progress

Several scouts completed requirements in First Aid, Cooking, and Hiking. We are proud of the effort and teamwork shown this month.

Upcoming May Fete Parade

Scouts will have the chance to walk our banner in the annual May Fete Parade. Our troop have partook in this parade in past years and are planning to run it back again, with the addition of one more race cart.
