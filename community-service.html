Service Projects

The service projects done by Eagle Scouts in Scouting America are large-scale projects for community service organized by the Scouts aspiring to become Eagles. The Scouts undertake projects that would help schools, nonprofit organizations, or local community organizations. Such projects show leadership qualities and willingness to serve others on the part of the Scouts.

How We Help

  • Neighborhood and creek cleanups
  • Community garden work days
  • Food and supply donation drives
  • Building and Construction projects
