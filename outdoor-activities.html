Camping

Camping trips teach scouts how to plan meals, set up tents, stay safe, and work together. Campfire time gives everyone a chance to relax, share stories, and celebrate teamwork.

Hiking

Hiking helps scouts build endurance and confidence while learning map skills and outdoor awareness. We respect nature by following trail rules and leaving each area cleaner than we found it.

Patrol Highlights

Propane Turtles

The Propane Turtles are steady and organized. They are known for strong camp cooking and helping new scouts feel welcome on outdoor trips.

Vipers

The Vipers bring lots of energy and are always ready for the next challenge. They enjoy learning trail navigation, first aid, and advanced knot skills.
