What to Expect

Summer camp gives scouts a full week outdoors with merit badge classes, swimming, campfire programs, and patrol competitions. It is a great way to practice independence and make new friends.

Sample Camp Schedule

  • Morning: Merit badge classes and skill instruction
  • Afternoon: Aquatics, hiking, and team games
  • Evening: Dinner, troop time, and campfire activities

Preparation Tips

Scouts should pack labeled gear, sturdy shoes, water bottles, and weather-ready layers. Being prepared helps everyone stay safe and enjoy camp.
